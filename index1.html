Speech is power: speech is to persuade, to convert, to compel. It is to bring another out of his bad sense into your good sense.



Diction is an assistive technology in which a computer or tablet can read the words on the screen out loud to the user. This technology will help people who have difficulties with reading, especially those who struggle with decoding. By presenting the words auditorily, they can focus on the meaning of words instead of spending all their brain power trying to sound out the words.
